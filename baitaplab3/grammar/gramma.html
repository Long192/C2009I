NOUNS

A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea

kinds of noun

  • Phoper: It is the name of a particular object, whether person, place, animal, ideal or thing.
  • Common: It is the name possessed by thing in general.
  • Collective: It is the name representing a group of things.
  • Abastract: It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions or action

PRONOUNS

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.

  • Personal pronouns: It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitite of the noun.
  • Interrogative pronouns: It stands as the answer to the question about a person, place or a thing.
  • Indefinite pronouns: It is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknow.
  • Relative pronouns: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word orexpression preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.
  • Demonstrative pronouns: A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it.
  • Reflexive pronouns: It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize subject.
